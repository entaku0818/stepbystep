StepByStep is a task management app that automatically breaks down tasks into 5 actionable steps using AI, enforcing single-task focus to help you stay concentrated and productive.

Core Features:

AI-powered task splitting (powered by Gemini API)
Single-task enforcement: only one active task at a time
Sequential execution: no skipping steps or multitasking
Minimal UI showing only current step for focus
Progress tracking with completion animations
Daily usage limits (3 AI splits per day for free version)

Key Benefits:

Simple text input with automatic AI breakdown
One task registration at a time
No way back until task completion
Minimal design supporting concentration
Perfect for ADHD or focus-challenged individuals

StepByStep helps you accomplish tasks through forced sequential execution and extreme simplicity. Experience focused, distraction-free productivity.
