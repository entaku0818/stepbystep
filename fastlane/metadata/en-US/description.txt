One Task Steps is a task management app that uses AI to automatically split tasks into 5 actionable steps, enforcing single-task focus.

Core Features:
- AI-powered task splitting (using Gemini API)
- Single-task enforcement: Only one task at a time
- Sequential execution: Cannot skip steps or multitask
- Shows only current step to maintain focus
- Progress tracking and completion animations

Highlights:
- Simple text input with AI auto-splitting
- Only one task can be registered at a time
- Cannot go back until step is completed
- Minimal UI to support focus
- 3 AI uses per day limit (free version)

Target Users:
Perfect for those with ADHD or focus challenges, and those who struggle with multitasking.

Simple design to support steady task execution.
